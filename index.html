Welcome to Jhalak!

Hosted by the Indian Cultural Association at The University of Texas at Austin, Jhalak is one of the only Bollywood Fusion dance competitions in America that removes production and props from the judging criteria. Every year, we invite 8 talented teams from around the nation to enjoy a memorable experience where they can cut the frills and bring the skills onstage. Our goal is to host a successful competition that allows teams to showcase the beauty of Bollywood Fusion, their talent, and their love for the art of dance. We aim to present our teams with a memorable experience and a true taste of Southern hospitality.

Learn about Jhalak

Legends is the official Bollywood Dance Championship founded by former dancers and competition organizers from the Desi Dance community. Jhalak has been an official Legends partner competition since its debut in 2016. Jhalak enjoys the opportunity to collaborate and innovate with this championship, as well as other fusion competitions across the country.

Bollywood America is the largest fusion championship in the world that brings together the best of the best Bollywood fusion dance teams to compete for the ultimate championship. Bollywood America was held on April 20, 2021, in Atlanta, GA. Jhalak enjoys the opportunity to collaborate and innovate with this championship, as well as other fusion competitions across the country.

Last year, we partnered with MannMukti. MannMukti is a storytelling platform, launched May 2017, that enables South Asians to normalize and discuss mental health issues. Their mission is to encourage healthy, open dialogue of mental health issues in an effort to remove stigma, improve awareness and promote self-care. If you are interested in learning more, please check out mannmukti.org.

Get Involved!

Explore the different ways you can be a part of the Jhalak experience.

COME COMPETE

Come and show your true talent without the use of any production, props, or theme! You can enjoy a memorable experience onstage while experiencing true Southern hospitality offstage. If you are interested in competing at Jhalak, please view our Registration Packet to learn more. The Regular Application deadline for all application materials is September 24, 2021!

Registration Packet

BE A LIAISON

Liaisons are the glue that hold teams together, connecting Jhalak's goals of hospitality, safety, fun, and integrity seamlessly that weekend. Ideal liaisons should be ready to foster meaningful relationships with their teams, making sure to keep responsibility, organization, communication, and enthusiasm top of mind. Jhalak 2021 Liaison Application coming soon!

Liaison Application

BE A VOLUNTEER

Come help your favorite Hospitality Committee Chairs put on the best national dance competition in the South! Volunteers will be involved in every aspect of Jhalak, from greeting teams at the hotel to being backstage during the show. This is a chance to get creative, get involved, and get a chance to experience something like never before. *Volunteer positions are restricted to UT Indian Cultural Association members. To join ICA and learn more about our organization, please visit www.utexas-ica.com!*
